Ginger Root Juice

A bold, refreshing blend of zesty lime and spicy ginger, this cold-pressed juice is your go-to pick-me-up. Packed with natural goodness to boost energy, support digestion, and awaken your senses—no additives, no nonsense.

Ingredients

Water, ginger root, lime, brown sugar, and mint.

How to enjoy

Add water if you’d like a lighter, more refreshing taste. Want it sweeter? A splash of your favorite sweetener does the trick. Chill before serving or pour over ice for the perfect cool sip.

Keep refrigerated and enjoy within a week of opening for the best taste and freshness.

Hibiscus Juice

About Mariama’s Juices

My name is Mariama-Cire Keita. Born in Paris and raised in Guinea, I’ve called Ottawa home since 1990. Inspired by my West African roots, I discovered the incredible health benefits of traditional juices made from ginger root, hibiscus flower, baobab, and kiri fruit. Rich in antioxidants and essential nutrients, these superfood drinks support immunity, digestion, and overall wellness. My mission is to share these natural, healing beverages with the world.
